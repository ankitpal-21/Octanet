Features of this Zoo

Tiger

The tiger, a majestic and powerful big cat, is known for its striking orange coat with black stripes. As an apex predator, it primarily inhabits forests and grasslands in Asia. Sadly, tigers face threats from habitat loss and poaching, making conservation efforts crucial for their survival.

About Us

Indian Zoo is dedicated to the care and conservation of wild animals. Our mission is to protect endangered species, rehabilitate injured wildlife, and provide a safe, natural habitat for all creatures. Through education and research, Indian Zoo strives to foster a deeper understanding and respect for the natural world. Join us in preserving our planet's biodiversity.
